Ingredients & Allergens

We believe in transparency about what goes into our mochi.

Ingredients

Mochi Shell: Glutinous rice flour, sugar, water, cornstarch

Ice Cream Filling: Milk, cream, sugar, egg yolks, vanilla extract, chocolate sandwich cookies (enriched flour, sugar, palm oil, cocoa powder, high fructose corn syrup, leavening agents, salt, soy lecithin, vanillin)

Allergen Warning: Contains dairy, eggs, wheat, soy. Made in a facility that also processes nuts.
