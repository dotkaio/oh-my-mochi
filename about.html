Our Story

Discover the passion and inspiration behind Oh My Mochi.

Our Mochi Journey

Oh My Mochi brings you handcrafted, small-batch mochi with authentic recipes inspired by Thailand. We fuse traditional flavors with a modern dessert experience, creating unique treats that delight with every bite.

At Oh My Mochi, we believe that every mochi should be a moment of pure joy. Our mission is to share the magic of Thai-inspired mochi with our community, one delicious bite at a time.

Our Beginning

Our journey began with a simple passion for creating the perfect mochi – soft, chewy, and bursting with flavor. Inspired by traditional Thai desserts and techniques, we set out to bring something unique to California.

What started as kitchen experiments and sharing treats with friends quickly grew into a beloved local business. We developed our recipes through countless iterations, perfecting the delicate balance of flavors and textures that make our mochi special.

Today, we continue to handcraft each mochi with the same care, attention to detail, and premium ingredients that defined our earliest batches. Our small-batch approach ensures that every mochi maintains its exceptional quality and taste.

Thai Inspiration

Our mochi is uniquely inspired by Thailand's rich culinary heritage. Traditional Thai desserts are known for their intricate preparation, vibrant colors, and complex flavor profiles that balance sweetness with other taste elements.

We've taken these traditional inspirations and fused them with the beloved Japanese mochi format, creating something truly special. Our recipes incorporate authentic Thai ingredients and techniques while maintaining the chewy, delightful texture that makes mochi so irresistible.

Each flavor we develop celebrates this cultural fusion, offering an experience that honors tradition while introducing something new and exciting to the dessert landscape.

Thai Culinary Influence

Thailand's culinary tradition is known for its harmonious blend of sweet, sour, salty, and spicy flavors. Traditional Thai desserts often feature ingredients like coconut milk, tropical fruits, pandan, and various types of rice flour – elements we incorporate into our unique mochi creations.

We've studied these traditional techniques and flavors, adapting them to create mochi that offers a new experience while honoring the culinary heritage that inspires us. Each bite is a celebration of cross-cultural appreciation and innovation.
