Product Description

Our Strawberry Miruku mochi represents the perfect harmony between Japanese tradition and modern flavor innovation. Each piece features a delicate, pillowy-soft mochi shell made from premium glutinous rice flour, filled with our signature strawberry cream that's crafted using real strawberry puree sourced from local farms.

The name "Miruku" comes from the Japanese word for milk, reflecting the creamy, indulgent texture of our filling. We've perfected the balance between the sweet, fruity strawberry flavor and the subtle earthiness of the mochi, creating a dessert that's both familiar and exotic.

Best enjoyed fresh, our Strawberry Miruku mochi should be consumed within 2-3 days of purchase for optimal texture and flavor.

Ingredients

Mochi Shell: Glutinous rice flour, sugar, water, cornstarch, natural vanilla extract

Strawberry Cream Filling: Heavy cream, strawberry puree, sugar, cream cheese, cornstarch, natural strawberry flavor, citric acid

Dusting: Cornstarch, powdered sugar

All ingredients are sourced from trusted suppliers and meet our high-quality standards. We use no artificial preservatives or colors.
