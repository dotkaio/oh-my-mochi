Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Oh My Mochi.

Do you offer delivery or shipping?

No, we are currently a pickup-only business. We do not offer delivery or shipping services at this time. Orders can be placed online and picked up at our location during pickup hours.

How far in advance should I place my order?

For same-day pickup, orders must be placed at least 3 hours in advance. For larger orders (party boxes or custom orders), we recommend ordering 24-48 hours in advance to ensure availability.

How long do mochi stay fresh?

Our mochi are best enjoyed within 24-48 hours of pickup for optimal freshness and texture. They can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. We do not recommend freezing our mochi.

Do you offer custom orders or catering?

Yes, we offer custom orders and catering for special events. Please contact us directly at simplymochi.ca@gmail.com with details about your event, desired flavors, and quantity at least 7 days in advance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay through our secure online checkout system powered by Stripe. We do not accept cash payments.
